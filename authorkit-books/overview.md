# Overview

## What is AuthorKit for Books?

AuthorKit for Books is a comprehensive book management module built into the AuthorKit WordPress plugin. Designed specifically for self-publishing authors, it provides a professional catalog system to showcase your books on your website without requiring technical knowledge or coding skills.

## Key Features

### Complete Book Management
Store and manage all essential book information including titles, descriptions, ISBNs, publication dates, page counts, and more. Each book supports 27+ custom fields to capture every detail readers want to know.

### Professional Display Options
Display your books beautifully using built-in templates and shortcodes. Show single books, create book grids, filter by genre or series, and customize the appearance to match your brand.

### Organized Taxonomies
Categorize your books using multiple classification systems:
- **Book Categories**: Organize by genre (Fantasy, Romance, Mystery, etc.)
- **Book Series**: Group related books into series with proper ordering
- **Age Ranges**: Tag books for appropriate reader age groups

### Retailer Integration
Link directly to major book retailers including Amazon, Barnes & Noble, Apple Books, Kobo, and Goodreads. Make it easy for readers to purchase from their preferred platform.

### SEO Optimization
Built-in schema.org markup ensures search engines understand your book data, improving discoverability and enabling rich snippets in search results.

### Coming Soon Support
Promote pre-release books with a dedicated "Coming Soon" status. Build anticipation and collect interest before your launch date.

## Free vs Pro

**Free Tier**: Manage up to 10 books - perfect for new authors building their catalog.

**Pro Tier**: Unlimited books plus advanced features for established authors with extensive catalogs.

## Who Should Use This Module?

AuthorKit for Books is ideal for:
- Self-published authors selling direct or through retailers
- Indie authors building their author platform
- Writers managing multiple series and genres
- Authors who want professional book pages without hiring a developer

## Getting Started

1. Add your first book from the WordPress admin dashboard
2. Fill in book details and upload a cover image
3. Use shortcodes to display books on pages or posts
4. Customize the appearance to match your website design

The Books module integrates seamlessly with WordPress, using familiar interfaces and workflows. If you can write a blog post, you can manage your book catalog.
