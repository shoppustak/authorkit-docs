Build your email list by delivering reader magnets (lead magnets for authors) directly from your WordPress site.

**Pro Feature:** AuthorKit Magnets is included in AuthorKit Pro and Pro Max licenses.

## What Are Reader Magnets?

Reader magnets (also called lead magnets) are free content you offer to readers in exchange for their email address.

**Common reader magnets for authors:**
- Short story prequel to your series
- First chapter of your next book
- Exclusive deleted scenes
- Character guide or map
- Bonus epilogue
- Novella in your series universe

**Goal:** Grow your email list so you can notify readers about new releases, sales, and promotions.

## Key Features

### 1. Upload & Deliver Files

**Supported formats:**
- EPUB (ebooks for Apple Books, Kobo, Nook)
- MOBI (ebooks for older Kindles)
- PDF (ebooks for any device)
- MP3/M4A (audiobook samples)
- ZIP (multiple files bundled)

**How it works:**
1. Upload your reader magnet file to WordPress
2. Create a magnet delivery page
3. Add shortcode to display signup form
4. Reader enters email → receives download link

### 2. Email Capture Forms

**Customizable forms with:**
- First name + email (required)
- Optional custom fields (genre preference, etc.)
- Double opt-in support (confirm email before download)
- GDPR-compliant consent checkboxes
- Customizable success messages

**Anti-spam protection:**
- Google reCAPTCHA integration
- Honeypot fields
- Rate limiting (prevent bot signups)

### 3. Download Tracking & Analytics

**See exactly how your magnets perform:**
- Total downloads per magnet
- Conversion rate (visits vs signups)
- Download sources (which pages/campaigns)
- Geographic data (where readers are)
- Device types (mobile vs desktop)

**Reports include:**
- Daily/weekly/monthly trends
- Top-performing magnets
- Abandoned signup rate
- Email delivery success rate

### 4. Email List Management

**Built-in subscriber management:**
- View all subscribers in WordPress
- Export to CSV for email service providers
- Segment by magnet (who downloaded what)
- Bulk actions (delete, export, tag)
- Search and filter subscribers

**Integration with email services:**
- Mailchimp
- ConvertKit
- MailerLite
- ActiveCampaign
- Any service with Zapier or webhook support

### 5. Delivery Methods

**Choose how readers receive their magnet:**

**Immediate download:**
- Reader submits form → instant download link
- Best for: Single PDFs, EPUBs
- Highest conversion (no friction)

**Email delivery:**
- Reader submits form → receives email with download link
- Best for: Double opt-in, building engagement
- Confirms email address is valid

**Hybrid:**
- Immediate download + follow-up email
- Best for: Maximum delivery success

### 6. Security & Anti-Piracy

**Protected downloads:**
- Unique download links (not guessable URLs)
- Link expiration (24-hour, 7-day, or custom)
- Download limits (1 download per email, or unlimited)
- IP address tracking
- Hotlink protection (prevent direct file linking)

**GDPR compliance:**
- Privacy policy integration
- Data export for users
- Right to deletion
- Cookie consent handling

## How It Works

### Reader Experience (Frontend)

1. Reader visits your magnet landing page
2. Sees compelling offer: "Get my FREE prequel short story!"
3. Enters name + email in form
4. Clicks "Get My Free Book"
5. (Optional) Confirms email via double opt-in link
6. Receives download link (instant or via email)
7. Downloads EPUB/MOBI/PDF to their device

**Entire process happens on YOUR site** - readers never leave your domain.

### Author Experience (Backend)

1. Upload reader magnet file to WordPress
2. Create magnet post (like creating a book or page)
3. Configure delivery settings (instant vs email)
4. Add shortcode to landing page
5. Publish and start collecting emails
6. Monitor downloads via analytics dashboard
7. Export subscribers to email service

**Time to launch:** 10 minutes from upload to live magnet.

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Series Starter

**Scenario:** You have a 10-book fantasy series. Offer Book 1 free to hook readers.

**Setup:**
- Upload Book 1 EPUB, MOBI, PDF
- Create landing page highlighting series
- Add magnet signup form
- Set to "immediate download" for lowest friction

**Result:** Reader gets Book 1 instantly, you get their email to promote Books 2-10.

### Use Case 2: Exclusive Content

**Scenario:** Offer deleted scenes or bonus content to newsletter subscribers.

**Setup:**
- Create PDF with 3 deleted scenes
- Gate it behind email signup
- Promote on social media and in book back matter
- Set to "email delivery" to build relationship

**Result:** Fans get exclusive content, you grow email list with engaged readers.

### Use Case 3: Launch Day Boost

**Scenario:** New book releasing next month. Build pre-launch hype.

**Setup:**
- Upload first 3 chapters as PDF preview
- Create magnet with countdown timer to release
- Offer preview in exchange for email
- Auto-tag subscribers in email service for launch campaign

**Result:** 500+ emails collected before launch = 100+ launch day sales.

### Use Case 4: Multi-Magnet Funnel

**Scenario:** Different magnets for different reader segments.

**Setup:**
- Magnet A: Prequel novella (romance readers)
- Magnet B: World map + character guide (fantasy readers)
- Magnet C: First chapter of standalone (thriller readers)
- Track which magnet each subscriber signed up for
- Send targeted emails based on interests

**Result:** Higher engagement because emails match reader preferences.

## Integration with Email Services

AuthorKit Magnets connects with popular email marketing platforms:

### Direct Integrations

**Mailchimp:**

- Tag by magnet downloaded
- Trigger welcome automation

**ConvertKit:**

No automation? No problem:
1. Export subscribers to CSV
2. Import to your email service manually
3. Segment by magnet if needed
