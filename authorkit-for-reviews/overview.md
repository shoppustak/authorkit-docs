# Overview

AuthorKit Reviews brings Amazon-style book reviews to your WordPress site, allowing readers to rate and review your books with a professional 5-star rating system. The module provides complete review management, moderation tools, and customizable display options to build social proof and engage your audience.

## What is AuthorKit Reviews?

AuthorKit Reviews is a comprehensive review system designed specifically for authors who want to showcase reader feedback on their WordPress site. Unlike basic comment systems, Reviews provides structured star ratings, verified badges, spam protection, and powerful moderation tools that give you complete control over what appears on your site.

## Key Features

**Star Rating System**: Readers can leave 1-5 star ratings with written reviews. The module automatically calculates average ratings and displays them prominently on your book pages.

**Review Moderation**: All reviews enter a pending state by default, giving you the opportunity to approve or reject submissions before they appear publicly. This protects your site from spam and inappropriate content.

**Verified Purchase Badges**: Mark reviews as "verified purchases" to highlight authentic feedback from readers who bought your books, building trust with potential buyers.

**Frontend Submission**: Readers submit reviews directly from your book pages using a clean, user-friendly form. No WordPress login required.

**Spam Protection**: Built-in honeypot fields and rate limiting prevent automated spam submissions while keeping the review process simple for legitimate readers.

**Email Notifications**: Receive instant notifications when new reviews are submitted, so you can moderate them promptly.

**Flexible Display Options**: Sort reviews by newest, highest rated, lowest rated, or most helpful. Control how many reviews display per page.

**Review Count Display**: Show the total number of reviews alongside average ratings to demonstrate social proof.

## Requirements

AuthorKit Reviews requires the following components to function:

- **AuthorKit Core**: The base plugin that provides core functionality
- **AuthorKit Books**: The books module that manages your book catalog
- **WordPress 5.8 or higher**: For compatibility with modern WordPress features
- **PHP 7.4 or higher**: For optimal performance and security

## Free vs Pro Tiers

**Free Tier**: Each book can receive up to 3 reviews. Perfect for authors just starting to gather feedback or those with a small catalog.

**Pro Tier**: Unlimited reviews per book. Essential for established authors building substantial social proof.

## Getting Started

After activating AuthorKit Reviews, navigate to AuthorKit > Reviews in your WordPress dashboard to configure settings, manage submissions, and moderate reviews. The module integrates seamlessly with your book pages, automatically adding review sections where readers can view existing reviews and submit their own.
