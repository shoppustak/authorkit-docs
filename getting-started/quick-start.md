# 5-Minute Quick Start Guide

Get your first book on your website in under 5 minutes!

## Prerequisites

- WordPress 5.0 or higher
- PHP 7.0 or higher
- Admin access to your WordPress site

## Step 1: Install AuthorKit Core (1 minute)

1. Log in to your WordPress admin panel
2. Navigate to **Plugins → Add New**
3. Search for "**AuthorKit**"
4. Click **Install Now** on "AuthorKit Core"
5. Click **Activate**

Core is now installed.

## Step 2: Install AuthorKit Books (1 minute)

1. Still on the **Plugins → Add New** page
2. Search for "**AuthorKit Books**"
3. Click **Install Now**
4. Click **Activate**

Books module is now ready.

## Step 3: Add Your First Book (2 minutes)

1. In your WordPress admin, look for **Books** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add New Book**
3. Fill in the basics:
   - **Title**: Your book's title
   - **Description**: Your book blurb (in the main editor)
   - **Book Cover**: Click "Set featured image" and upload your cover
4. Scroll down to the **Book Details** meta box
5. Fill in key information:
   - **ISBN/ASIN**
   - **Publication Date**
   - **Pages**
   - **Price** (e.g., $2.99)
6. Add **Purchase Links**:
   - Amazon URL
   - Other retailer URLs
7. Click **Publish**

Your first book is live.

## Step 4: View Your Book (30 seconds)

1. Click the **View Book** link after publishing
2. Your book will display with all the information you entered
3. Beautiful Amazon-style layout automatically applied!

## Step 5: Display Books on Your Site (30 seconds)

**Add a book grid to any page/post:**

1. Edit any page or post
2. Add this shortcode:
   ```
   [authorkit_books count="6" columns="3"]
   ```
3. Update/Publish the page
4. View your page to see your books in a grid!

Your books are now showcased on your site.
