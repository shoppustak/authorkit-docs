## Free vs Pro Features

### What's in Free?

AuthorKit Free includes everything you need to get started:

- **Books module** - Up to 10 books with 27 custom fields
- **Reviews module** - Up to 3 reviews per book
- **Bookshelf sync** - Publish to bookshelf.authorkit.pro for discoverability
- **Amazon-style displays** - Professional book cards and layouts
- **SEO optimization** - Basic schema markup
- **Community support** - WordPress.org forums

**Perfect for:**
- Authors just starting their website
- Writers with small book catalogs (under 10 books)
- Testing AuthorKit before upgrading

### What's in Pro?

AuthorKit Pro unlocks everything:

- **Unlimited books** - No 10-book limit
- **Unlimited reviews** - No 3-review-per-book limit
- **Contests module** - Run book giveaways and promotions
- **Reader Magnets module** - Deliver lead magnets and grow your email list
- **Enhanced SEO** - Rich JSON-LD schema markup
- **Priority support** - Email support with 24-hour response time
- **Automatic updates** - No manual downloads

**Perfect for:**
- Professional authors with growing catalogs
- Writers running promotions and giveaways
- Authors serious about email list building

### What's in Pro Max?

Everything in Pro, but for **5 WordPress sites** instead of 1.

**Perfect for:**
- Independent publishers managing multiple author websites
- Book coaches building sites for clients
- Authors with multiple pen names/brands
- Agencies serving author clients

### What is Founding Member Pricing?

**Founding Member Benefits:**

- Price never increases on renewals

**Example (Pro Annual):**

## Annual vs Lifetime: Which Should You Choose?

### Cost Over Time

**Pro (1 site):**
- Annual: $49/year × 5 years = $245 (founding) or $79 × 5 = $395 (regular)
- Lifetime: $149 (founding) or $249 (regular) — pay once
- **Break-even point:** 3 years (founding), 3.1 years (regular)

**Pro Max (5 sites):**
- Annual: $99/year × 5 years = $495 (founding) or $149 × 5 = $745 (regular)
- Lifetime: $249 (founding) or $399 (regular) — pay once
- **Break-even point:** 2.5 years (founding), 2.7 years (regular)

### When to Choose Annual

Choose annual if you:
- Want lower upfront cost ($49 vs $149)
- Aren't sure about long-term commitment
- Prefer spreading costs over time
- Might change website platforms in 2-3 years

### When to Choose Lifetime

Choose lifetime if you:
- Plan to use WordPress long-term (3+ years)
- Want to avoid recurring payments
- Prefer one-time purchases over subscriptions
- Want maximum value over time

**Most popular:** Lifetime (like Vellum and Atticus, you buy it and keep it)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade from Free to Pro anytime?

**Yes!** No data loss. Your existing books and reviews remain intact. Limits are simply removed.

### Can I upgrade from Annual to Lifetime?

**Yes!** Contact support@authorkit.pro and we'll credit your annual payment toward lifetime.

### Can I upgrade from Pro (1 site) to Pro Max (5 sites)?

**Yes!** Pay the difference and we'll upgrade your license to Pro Max.

### What happens if I don't renew my Annual license?

- Pro features continue working
- Updates stop after license expires
- Renew anytime to resume updates
- Your books and data are never affected

### Can I downgrade from Pro to Free?

**Yes.** Deactivate your license and you'll revert to Free tier. Books 11+ and reviews 4+ are hidden (not deleted). Reactivate Pro anytime to restore them.

### Do founding member prices apply to renewals?

**Yes!** If you purchase at founding member pricing, your renewal rate stays at the founding price forever (as long as license remains active).

### Can I transfer my license between sites?

**Yes.** Deactivate on the old site, activate on the new site. See [Multi-Site Activation Guide](multi-site-activation.md).

### Is there a refund policy?

**Yes.** 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked.
