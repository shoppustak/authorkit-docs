# AuthorKit Bookshelf Overview

AuthorKit Bookshelf is a free book discovery platform that allows authors to showcase their books on bookshelf.authorkit.pro. It's a curated catalog where readers can browse and discover books from indie authors worldwide.

## What is AuthorKit Bookshelf?

AuthorKit Bookshelf connects your WordPress site to a centralized book discovery platform at [bookshelf.authorkit.pro](https://bookshelf.authorkit.pro). When enabled, you can choose which books from your catalog to publish to the public bookshelf, making them discoverable to readers searching for new books to read.

## Key Features

### For Authors
- **Free Discoverability**: Get your books in front of readers actively looking for new books
- **Control What You Share**: Choose which books to publish publicly
- **Direct Traffic**: All book listings link back to your author website
- **Genre-Based Discovery**: Books are organized by genre, series, and other taxonomies
- **Author Branding**: Your author name and website are prominently displayed

### For Readers
- **Advanced Filtering**: Search by genre, format, age range, and more
- **Series Discovery**: Find complete book series in one place
- **Direct Purchase Links**: One-click access to buy books on Amazon, Apple Books, etc.
- **Clean Interface**: Amazon-style book browsing experience

## How It Works

1. **Enable Bookshelf**: Turn on the Bookshelf module in your AuthorKit dashboard
2. **Select Books**: Choose which books from your catalog to publish publicly
3. **Automatic Sync**: Your books appear on bookshelf.authorkit.pro instantly
4. **Get Discovered**: Readers find your books through search and filtering
5. **Drive Traffic**: Book listings link back to your website for more information

## Requirements

- AuthorKit Core 1.0+
- Active WordPress installation
- At least one published book in your catalog

## Privacy & Control

- You choose exactly which books to share publicly
- "Coming Soon" books can be excluded automatically
- You can unpublish books at any time
- All book metadata is pulled from your site
- No reader data is collected or shared

## Cost

AuthorKit Bookshelf is **completely free** for all authors. There are no fees, subscriptions, or premium tiers. It's our way of supporting the indie author community.
