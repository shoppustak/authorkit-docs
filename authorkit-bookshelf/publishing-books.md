# Publishing Books to Bookshelf

Learn how to publish your books to bookshelf.authorkit.pro and make them discoverable to readers worldwide.

## Enable AuthorKit Bookshelf

1. Go to **AuthorKit → Dashboard** in your WordPress admin
2. Find the **AuthorKit Bookshelf** card
3. Toggle the switch to **Enabled**
4. Click **Save Settings**

The Bookshelf module is now active and ready to use.

## Configure Bookshelf Settings

1. Navigate to **AuthorKit → Bookshelf Settings**
2. Configure the following options:

### Auto-Publish Settings
- **Auto-publish new books**: Automatically publish new books when created
- **Exclude Coming Soon books**: Don't publish books with "Coming Soon" status
- **Default visibility**: Set default public/private status for new books

### Author Information
- **Author Name**: Your public author name (pulled from site settings)
- **Author Website**: Your website URL (pulled from site URL)
- **Author Bio**: Optional short bio to display with your books

### Book Metadata
The following book data is automatically synced:
- Title, subtitle, description
- Cover image
- ISBN (if provided)
- Publication date
- Genres and categories
- Series information
- Buy links (Amazon, Apple Books, etc.)
- Price information

## Publish Individual Books

### From the Book Editor

1. Edit any book in **Books → All Books**
2. Find the **Bookshelf Settings** meta box
3. Check **Publish to Bookshelf**
4. Click **Update** to save

The book will appear on bookshelf.authorkit.pro within a few minutes.

### Bulk Publishing

1. Go to **Books → All Books**
2. Select multiple books using checkboxes
3. Choose **Publish to Bookshelf** from the Bulk Actions dropdown
4. Click **Apply**

All selected books will be published to the public bookshelf.

## Unpublish Books

To remove a book from the public bookshelf:

1. Edit the book
2. Uncheck **Publish to Bookshelf**
3. Click **Update**

The book will be removed from bookshelf.authorkit.pro within a few minutes. It remains on your website.

## Update Published Books

When you edit a book that's already published to Bookshelf:

1. Make your changes to the book details
2. Click **Update**
3. Changes sync automatically to bookshelf.authorkit.pro

There's no need to unpublish and republish.

## What Gets Published?

The following information is shared publicly:

### Always Published
- Book title and subtitle
- Book description
- Cover image
- Author name
- Publication date
- Genres and categories
- Buy links

### Conditionally Published
- Series information (if book is part of a series)
- ISBN (if provided)
- Price (if provided)
- Page count (if provided)

### Never Published
- Your WordPress admin email
- Book draft content
- Private notes or internal metadata
- Unpublished books

## View Your Published Books

To see how your books appear on Bookshelf:

1. Visit [bookshelf.authorkit.pro](https://bookshelf.authorkit.pro)
2. Search for your author name or book titles
3. Click on any book to see the full listing

Each book listing includes:
- Cover image and title
- Author name (links to your website)
- Book description
- Buy buttons for all your retail links
- Genre and category tags

## Troubleshooting

### Book Not Appearing on Bookshelf

**Check the following:**
- Book status is "Published" (not Draft)
- "Publish to Bookshelf" is checked
- Bookshelf module is enabled
- Your site can connect to authorkit.pro API

**Wait a few minutes** - it can take up to 5 minutes for new books to appear.

### Book Information Is Incorrect

1. Edit the book in WordPress
2. Update the incorrect information
3. Click **Update**
4. Changes sync within 5 minutes

### Want to Remove All Books

1. Go to **AuthorKit → Bookshelf Settings**
2. Click **Unpublish All Books**
3. Confirm the action

All your books will be removed from bookshelf.authorkit.pro. They remain on your website.

## Best Practices

### Optimize Your Book Listings

1. **Use high-quality cover images** - At least 1600x2400px
2. **Write compelling descriptions** - Hook readers in the first sentence
3. **Set accurate genres** - Help readers find your books
4. **Include all buy links** - Give readers multiple purchase options
5. **Complete series information** - Makes it easy for readers to find related books

### Keep Listings Updated

- Update book information when you make changes
- Add new buy links as they become available
- Update prices during promotions

### Monitor Your Listings

- Search for your books on bookshelf.authorkit.pro regularly
- Check that all information displays correctly
- Ensure cover images load properly
