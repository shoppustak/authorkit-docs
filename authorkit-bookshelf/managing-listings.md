# Managing Your Bookshelf Listings

Learn how to organize, update, and optimize your books on bookshelf.authorkit.pro.

## View All Published Books

To see which books are currently published to Bookshelf:

1. Go to **AuthorKit → Bookshelf**
2. View the **Published Books** tab

This shows all books currently visible on bookshelf.authorkit.pro with their sync status.

## Update Book Information

### Sync Individual Books

To update a single book's information on Bookshelf:

1. Edit the book in **Books → All Books**
2. Make your changes
3. Click **Update**

Changes sync automatically within 5 minutes. No additional action needed.

### Force Sync

If changes don't appear after 5 minutes:

1. Go to **AuthorKit → Bookshelf**
2. Find the book in the list
3. Click **Sync Now**

This forces an immediate sync of that book's data.

### Bulk Update

To sync multiple books at once:

1. Go to **AuthorKit → Bookshelf**
2. Select books using checkboxes
3. Choose **Sync Selected** from Bulk Actions
4. Click **Apply**

## Organize Your Listings

### By Genre

Books on Bookshelf are automatically organized by the genres you've assigned in WordPress:

1. Edit a book
2. Assign **Book Categories** (these become genres on Bookshelf)
3. Update the book

Readers can filter by genre on bookshelf.authorkit.pro.

### By Series

If your books are part of a series:

1. Assign books to a **Book Series** taxonomy
2. Set the **Book Number in Series** meta field
3. Update each book

Books in the same series will be grouped together on Bookshelf.

### By Format

Set the **Book Format** meta field (eBook, Paperback, Hardcover, Audiobook) for each book. Readers can filter by format.

## Manage Visibility

### Temporarily Hide Books

To temporarily remove a book from Bookshelf without deleting it:

1. Edit the book
2. Uncheck **Publish to Bookshelf**
3. Click **Update**

The book is removed from bookshelf.authorkit.pro but remains on your site.

### Coming Soon Books

Books marked as "Coming Soon" can be excluded automatically:

1. Go to **AuthorKit → Bookshelf Settings**
2. Check **Exclude Coming Soon books**
3. Save settings

Coming Soon books won't appear on Bookshelf until you change their status.

## View Analytics

Track how your books are performing on Bookshelf:

1. Go to **AuthorKit → Bookshelf → Analytics**
2. View metrics for each book:
   - Views on bookshelf.authorkit.pro
   - Clicks to your website
   - Click-through rate

**Note**: Analytics are aggregate only. No individual reader data is collected.

## Update Author Information

Your author name and website appear on all your book listings. To update:

1. Go to **AuthorKit → Bookshelf Settings**
2. Update **Author Name** and **Author Website**
3. Click **Save Settings**

Changes apply to all your published books immediately.

## Handle Unpublished Books

Books removed from Bookshelf:
- Are deleted from bookshelf.authorkit.pro within 15 minutes
- Remain fully intact on your WordPress site
- Can be republished at any time

To see recently unpublished books:

1. Go to **AuthorKit → Bookshelf**
2. Click the **Unpublished** tab
3. View recently removed books

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Book Shows Old Information

**Solution**: Force sync the book
1. **AuthorKit → Bookshelf**
2. Find the book
3. Click **Sync Now**

### Cover Image Not Updating

**Solution**: Clear your book's image cache
1. Re-upload the cover image with a new filename
2. Update the book
3. Wait 5 minutes for sync

### Buy Links Not Working

**Solution**: Check your book's buy link URLs
1. Edit the book
2. Verify all URLs in **Buy Links** meta fields are correct
3. Ensure links start with `https://`
4. Update the book

### Book Not Appearing in Search

**Solution**: Check genre and metadata
1. Ensure book has at least one **Book Category** assigned
2. Verify **Book Title** and **Author Name** are set
3. Force sync the book

## Best Practices

### Keep Metadata Current
- Update prices during promotions
- Add new buy links as they become available
- Remove dead links promptly

### Use High-Quality Images
- Cover images should be at least 1600x2400px
- Use JPG or PNG format
- Keep file size under 2MB for fast loading

### Complete All Fields
Books with complete information rank higher in search:
- Full description (at least 150 words)
- Genre/categories
- Publication date
- Buy links for all available formats
- Series information (if applicable)

### Monitor Regularly
Check your listings monthly:
- Verify information is accurate
- Test buy links
- Review how your covers display

## Bulk Actions

Available bulk actions for published books:

- **Sync Selected** - Force update multiple books
- **Unpublish Selected** - Remove multiple books from Bookshelf
- **Update Genre** - Change genre for multiple books
- **Update Author** - Change author name for multiple books
